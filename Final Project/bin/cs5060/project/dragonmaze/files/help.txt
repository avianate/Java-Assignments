
Sir Herold the Hero is stuck in an evil dragon's maze. Help Sir Herold the Hero find 
his way through the labyrinth to get the key and escape. Look for the energy drink 
to help Sir Herold the Hero double his pace!


HOW TO PLAY:

Move the Hero 'H' to find the key 'K' to unlock the door 'E' and escape.
Pick up an energy drink 'e' to move the hero twice per turn for 10 turns.

press 'l' to move LEFT
press 'r' to move RIGHT
press 'u' to move UP
press 'd' to move DOWN
press 'w' to SKIP A TURN

press 'q' to QUIT
press 'h' for HELP
